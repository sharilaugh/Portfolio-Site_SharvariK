Graphic Case Study

Product Catalog

SKILLS

    Multi-page Layout
  • Photo Manipulation
  • Photo Composite
  • Typography
  • Pre-press

TOOLS & SOFTWARES

    InDesign
  • Photoshop
  • Canon Rebel
  • Acrobat

A product catalog is necessary for product businesses that have numerous products. Print design is one of my favorite mediums to make. All the decisions such as color, fontsize, pages make it worthwhile once you finally hold the creation in your hand.

This catalog was a different work due it to being based entirely on products. I had to make it crisp, technically and beautiful. The images were not pretty things or landscapes but just boring cameras. I decided to work with the tech team to take apart some products to photograph all the layers making it up. Once in a while a technical photograph of an product needed to be edited (such as the inside of a lens, the back port of a dvr) so it took away from the endless line of products displayed. The colors and fonts were used to make it as sharp and formal to contrast with some of the real-time use imagery.
